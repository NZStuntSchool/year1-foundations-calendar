🐴 Horse Lessons — 12 Hours Included
12 hours of horse lessons are included throughout the year, booked on weekdays at Hunua. We'll coordinate session times with you directly to suit your availability.
What's Included in Year 1
✔ Water Workshop — 1 session
✔ Wires — 2 sessions + repeats
✔ Car Hits — 1 session
✔ Fire Burns — 1 session
✔ Air Ram — 1 session
✔ High Falls — 1 session
✔ Horse Work — 12 hours
✔ Monthly Block Training — all year
✔ Open Mat — all year
✔ Free NZAT Joining Fee
Sessions may occasionally be postponed or cancelled due to weather or instructor availability. Alternative dates will always be offered.
Beyond the Schedule
📊Ongoing assessment throughout the year — feedback and tips to track your growth
🎬Block training across four scenario themes — Urban Combat, War & Battle, Pursuit & Escape, Epic Action
🏋️Free time to develop body control and complement your training with martial arts or sports alongside the programme
📋Grant application support available — ask us when you register
Please note: This is a draft schedule for 2027 and is subject to change due to instructor or location availability. If you cannot attend a workshop from your programme, an alternative date will always be offered. · You must complete Fight for Camera (FFC) before joining the Year Programme — either in person or online. If you have already attended a similar beginner workshop, you may be assessed and join directly without completing FFC. Contact us to discuss.
